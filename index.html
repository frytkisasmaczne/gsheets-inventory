How to use

  1. Set your Google OAuth client ID and connect your account.
  2. Choose or create a scan sheet and a master reference sheet.
  3. Open the app on your phone, start the camera, and scan QR codes.
  4. If a code is duplicate, the app warns you immediately.
  5. The scan sheet grows automatically with each new code.
